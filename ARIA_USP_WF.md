> *"Wells Fargo built a workbench. ARIA built a thinking partner."*

---

## Research Summary: Wells Fargo Advisor Technology

### Their Stack
- **Advisor Gateway** — single sign-on hub with 200+ tools and applications
- **Proprietary tools:** Account 360, Client Review Center (CRC), Proposal & Portfolio Analytics (PPA), Envision (Monte Carlo financial planning)
- **Third-party integrations:** Salesforce (CRM), eMoney (planning), FactSet (research), Black Diamond (reporting), Morningstar Workstation, iCapital (alternatives)
- **AI investment:** $1B+ in advisor platform, but AI is enterprise-wide (Tachyon, Google Agentspace) — NOT advisor-workflow-specific
- **Key gap vs peers:** Morgan Stanley's AI @ Debrief (98% advisor adoption, meeting transcription + CRM auto-fill) and Merrill's "ask MERRILL" (23M interactions) are purpose-built for advisors. Wells Fargo has no equivalent as of March 2026.

## ARIA's Core USP

> **"Wells Fargo built a workbench. ARIA built a thinking partner."**

WF gives advisors 200+ tools and expects them to synthesize it.
ARIA does the synthesis — surfaces who needs attention, why, and what to say before the RM picks up the phone.

---

## Three Angles to Lead With

### 1. Speed to Insight
Morning Briefing + AI Situation Summary means an RM walks into every client call prepared — without digging through 5 systems. Zero context-switching.

### 2. AI That Explains Itself
Every recommendation has plain-language Claude-generated rationale baked in. This is a compliance differentiator (SEBI/SEC audit-ready) and a client trust builder. WF's AI tools don't do this.

### 3. Middleware, Not Replacement
ARIA sits on top of any core banking system — no rip-and-replace. The pitch to a bank: *"Your CBS was built to process transactions, not to think. ARIA thinks."*

## The 10-Minute Demo Scenario (North Star)

1. RM opens ARIA in the morning — AI surfaces 5 clients flagged for attention
2. Clicks on Priya Sharma — Client 360 loads, AI Situation Summary auto-generates
3. RM asks: *"She just told me she needs the money in 6 months, what changes?"*

5. RM reviews, approves recommendation
6. Client receives plain-language explanation
7. Audit log captures the entire chain with AI rationale

**WF cannot do steps 3–7. Morgan Stanley can do step 3 only.**
